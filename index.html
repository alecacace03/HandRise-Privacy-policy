HandRise Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of the HandRise: Hand Recovery iPad application.

Privacy-First Approach: Our app is designed to function without collecting any user data.

1. Data Collection

The HandRise: Hand Recovery app does not collect personal data. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect or store names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location, your contacts, or your activity within the app.
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that may collect your data.

2. Local Storage

Any data or settings created within the app are stored exclusively on your device (local storage). This data is never transmitted to our servers or to any third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
